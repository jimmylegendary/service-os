# PORTFOLIO.md

## Active services

### 1. Today It
Status: primary focus

A today-first action system.
Not a traditional scheduler or backlog-heavy todo manager.
Its purpose is to compress mental clutter into a small, actionable present surface.

Why this matters:
- strongest product thesis right now
- directly usable and dogfoodable by Jimmy
- likely foundation for later multi-user and human-AI coordination patterns

Why now:
- clearest problem/vision fit
- reusable structural inheritance from MissionAmongUs exists
- best first proving ground for agent-assisted product building

### 2. Study It
Status: secondary / dependent on Today It foundations

A purpose-driven study and goal-oriented group coordination app.
More operational than social.
Likely to inherit task and flow concepts from Today It.

Why this matters:
- addresses goal-oriented groups poorly served by social/community apps
- extends Today It from personal execution to shared execution

Why not now:
- depends on task, state, and coordination concepts that Today It should clarify first

### 3. AI workflow as API service
Status: exploratory / platform layer

A platform for exposing AI workflows as reusable services so creators can monetize them and users can consume narrow-fit AI workflows.
Should likely be approached after internal workflow execution patterns are understood in practice.

Why this matters:
- aligns with long-term ecosystem and monetization direction
- leverages n8n, MCP, and agent orchestration capabilities already present

Why not now:
- platform complexity is too high before narrower products are validated
- better approached after real internal workflow execution lessons are accumulated

## Longer-term directions

- progressive automation of product creation and operation
- MCP/API-mediated human-AI task exchange
- reusable product-building and deployment protocols
- service factory / operating model for multi-product work

## Current strategy

1. establish central design and working system
2. analyze prior product attempts for reusable structure
3. define Today It philosophy and MVP sharply
4. create Today It implementation repo
5. use Today It as the first proving ground for agent-assisted product development
